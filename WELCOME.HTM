Welcome! 

The purpose of this page is to attempt to show the different branches of the Dri family, whose origin is in Friuli in northeastern Italy and whose descendants are to be found in many parts of the world.   

Since the web page has been fashioned in Argentina, the information we have available about the branches of the descendants in the United States and in English speaking countries, as well as information about common ancestors, is as yet scarce.  For this reason we hope that those of you who visit our site will provide us with the data that you have been able to obtain so that all the family ties possible can be established between the various descendants. 

It is my sincerest wish that all the Dri who visit this page will communicate with us and provide us with information about any branch of the family which has had its origins in Friuli. 

Many thanks to all of you!!! And please keep your letters coming. 

With warmest brotherly regards...
